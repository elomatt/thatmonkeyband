  • About the Band

    Monkey See Monkey Do met in bustling the folk scene of Southampton, where Aaron and Sam grew up and Helen and Matt studied.

    After many well-spent evenings at sessions (and other excuses to pass time in pubs), the current line-up came together in 2015. A blend of tunes and songs both ancient and modern is fused with unique energy to get feet tapping and faces smiling!
